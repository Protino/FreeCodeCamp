LEONARDO DA VINCI

  • 15 April 1452

    He takes birth "at the third hour of the night" in the Tuscan hill town of Vinci, in the lower valley of the Arno river in the territory of the Medici-rules Republic of Florence.

    Read more
  • 1466

    At the age of fourteen is apprenticed to the artist Andra di Clione, known as Verrocchio, whose workshop was "one of the finest in Florence", as a studio boy. Collaborates with Verrocchio to complete his first art work - "The Baptism of Christ". Also completes his own pen and ink painting of the Arno valley.

    Read more
  • 1472-1475

    Paints the Annunciation with Andrea del Verrocchio.

    Read more
  • 1476

    Paints the Portrait of Ginevra de' Benci a lady of aristocratic class in 15th century Florence.

    Read more
  • 1978

    Paints "The Benois Madonna" - Madonna and Child with Flowers.
